Do NOT start implementing anything yet.

Your only job right now is to talk to me. Ask me questions. Clarify what I want. Surface assumptions. Resolve ambiguities. Propose approaches if it helps, but don't commit to one without my sign-off.

When — and only when — we both agree the task is clearly understood, tell me: "OK, I've got it. Starting now." Then begin.
